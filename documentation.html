    Overview

    What is Pricey about?

    Naturally, people like to get the best deals when making purchases and it could be really frustrating and painful to realize they can get a cheaper deal for a product they had spent some extra thousands of naira on. For this singular reason, it is ideal to have a guide as regards the best available price for a particular product that interests the user. In modern age, people tend to consult the internet before making final decision on their purchases. Pricey provides luxury market prices on gadgets and electronics of various brands to safe online shoppers’ time money and also facilitates swift decision making. Pricey is a website that allows shoppers to search and compare prices of various gadgets from multiple retailers.

    How do we make money?

    When you follow our links to visit a store, we get paid a commission on what you buy during your visit. We then share that commission with you, our members. The commission amount are based on commission rates that the participating store offers at the time of purchase. Retailers pay us to drive sales and help grow their brands. We relay shoppers to their websites or brick-and-mortar stores and may also feature them on our website, in emails, or other marketing opportunities.

    Navigating Pricey

    Pricey's interface is divided into 4 areas:

    • Header - contains the actions of the website, Home, products, contacts, about and FAQs and your profile.
    • Sidebar - Filter search and subscribe to pricey’s newsletter
    • Main pane - displays a list of the available categories, top deals,top products, contact us,store directory and top brands.
    • Footer - contains the links to pricey’s social media, list your online shop, download the pricey app and many more.

    Your Account

    There are two types of users in Pricey; the authenticated users and the unauthenticated users.

    The unauthenticated users are the users that do not own an account with Pricey. Even though they do not have an account, these people can still use the website, however their activities are limited as they do not gain full access to the website.

    As an unauthenticated user, you will be able to perfom the following functions on Pricey,com ;

  • Visit the website and view basic information
  • View the documentation
  • Browse through the products available on the website.
  • To view more details about these products and also to gain full access to Pricey, these unauthenticated users have to register with Pricey.
    •

    When the unauntheticated users create an account, they become authenticated users of Pricey and they gain full access to the website.

    FAQs

    Can I use Pricey without creating an account?

    Yes, you can use Pricey without creating an account,. However, you have limited access to some of our best features.

    Will Pricey be responsible for any delivery issues that may occur with vendors?

    Pricey will not be responsible for any issues that may arise during purchase of prodcuts, including delivery. Pricey only helps users compare the prices of products from different vendors, we are not responsible for the sale of these products. However, we at Pricey, ensure that the vendors we connect our users to are legitimate, certified, and registered. But we will not be held responsible for any purchasing issues.

    Does Pricey offer discount on products?

    Pricey cannot offer discounts, only the vendors of the products can offer discounts. However, Pricey will inform you of discounts or black friday sales from the vendors.

    Can I buy directly from Pricey?

    You can compare the prices of products from major retailers like Amazon, Walmart, eBay, after that you will be redirected to their corresponding websites for purchase, when you click on “Shop Now” button.

    Can I use Pricey on my phone and tablet?

    Yes, this website is responsive, You can use the website on your phone, desktop, and tablet.

    Is Pricey free to use?

    Yes, Pricey is 100% free. You do not need to pay any subscription fee or extra cost on your purchases.
